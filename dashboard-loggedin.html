Who are we?

Chow Town is a technology company that connects restaurants with hungry customers in their area. By facilitating delivery and pickup orders, Chow Town helps restaurants grow their takeout sales, reach new customers, and increase brand awareness online.


How can I order?

It's simple! Just enter your address, click 'Create your order,' select your desired restaurants and items, and checkout for delivery or take-out!
